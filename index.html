Mark Zuckerberg

Co-founder of Facebook and CEO of Meta Platforms. A visionary in technology and digital communication, he has reshaped how the world connects.

Key Achievements

Founded Facebook

Launched Facebook in 2004 from his Harvard dorm, transforming social media forever.

Global Expansion

Facebook became the world's largest social network with over 2.9 billion active users.

Meta and Virtual Reality

Rebranded Facebook to Meta, focusing on AR/VR and building the metaverse.

Chan Zuckerberg Initiative

Founded in 2015 with his wife, supporting health, education, and scientific research.

Development Timeline

  • 2004: Launched Facebook from Harvard.
  • 2012: Acquired Instagram.
  • 2014: Bought WhatsApp and Oculus VR.
  • 2021: Facebook rebranded to Meta Platforms Inc.

Relevant Personal Information

Full Name: Mark Elliot Zuckerberg

Born: May 14, 1984, White Plains, New York

Education: Harvard University (did not graduate)

Family: Married to Priscilla Chan, 3 children

Famous Quote: "The biggest risk is not taking any risk."

Interests: Technology, AI, reading, programming

Current Role: CEO of Meta Platforms Inc.
